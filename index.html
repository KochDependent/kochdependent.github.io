Our Story - as told by the bride

How We Met

We met at a friends birthday party and I impressed him over my craft beer knowledge and tequila shots. We then wound up talking all night. The better story actually is that a few days after this party I came back up to Cleveland to celebrate New Years with some friends and Mike happened to be celebrating New Years at the same place. We had been texting prior to this and planned to meet up that night. He actually ended up being my New Years Kiss! Mike went to get a drink and while he was gone the clock started to count down and he was no where to be found and with just seconds to go he comes up behind me and says, "you didn't think I would miss this did you?" and then we kissed right at midnight. It was very magical and makes for a great story!

The Proposal

We were in Boston for the weekend visiting Boston College for Mike's admited student day for law school. Mike booked this hotel room for the night that had our own private balcony with a skyline view of the city. We went to dinner at this nice place. When we first sat down our server poured us a glass of champaign. I immediatly started getting suspicious, but mike assured me that he had no idea what was going on. Later they brought out really nice tequlia with an orange slice (just like what we bonded over the first night we met) and I grew even more suspicious. Then they brought out a side of mac and cheese with a carrot on top (1. mac and cheese is my favorite food and 2. the carrot is from and inside joke). At that point I asked Mike what was going on and he re-directed the conversation away from the topic. We then has dessert and left the restaurant!!! He didn't purpose there so at this point I was really confused. We then got back to the hotel room and there waiting for us is a bottle of champaign and a tray of chocolate covered strawberries. I knew right at that point what was going on and started to tear up. Mike poured us a glass of champaign and took me out onto the balcony. At this point, the Boston skyline was all lit up at night in front of us. Mike then started to tell me how much he loved me and got down on one knee. At this point I was crying and of course said yes!
